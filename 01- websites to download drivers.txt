Chrome:
System.setProperty("webdriver.chrome.driver", your driver path in String);
https://chromedriver.chromium.org/downloads

Firefox:
System.setProperty("webdriver.gecko.driver", your driver path in String);
https://github.com/mozilla/geckodriver/releases

Edge:
System.setProperty("webdriver.edge.driver", your driver path in String);
https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/tools/webdriver/#downloads

Opera:
System.setProperty("webdriver.opera.driver", your driver path in String);
https://github.com/operasoftware/operachromiumdriver/releases

internet explorer
System.setProperty("webdriver.ie.driver", your driver path in String);
https://github.com/SeleniumHQ/selenium/wiki/InternetExplorerDriver#required-configuration

=================================================================

to get the required website for any driver, you need to
1- go to this website
https://www.selenium.dev/downloads/
2- scroll down to this part "Platforms Supported by Selenium"
    and click on + button beside "Browsers"
